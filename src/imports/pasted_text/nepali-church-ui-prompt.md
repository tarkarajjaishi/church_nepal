Image Style

Use authentic, diverse images of Nepali Christian churches and congregations, including worship services, prayer, fellowship, youth gatherings, children's ministry, baptisms, music teams, and church outreach. Avoid generic corporate stock photos. Use warm natural lighting, joyful expressions, and culturally appropriate clothing and architecture where possible.

Expected Result

Create a premium, world-class church website UI comparable in quality to leading international church websites, while reflecting the culture and needs of Nepali churches. The design should be clean, welcoming, emotionally engaging, easy to navigate, and ready for implementation with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and shadcn/ui.
